The World of Coffee

I plan to create a website focused on coffee and coffee culture. The website will provide information about different types of coffee beans, brewing methods, coffee equipment, and drink recipes. It will also include guides for beginners, coffee reviews, and tips for making better coffee at home. The site will be designed for coffee enthusiasts who want to learn more about coffee and improve their brewing skills. By the end of the semester, the website will contain multiple pages covering topics such as coffee origins, brewing techniques, equipment recommendations, recipes, and coffee shop reviews.
