The World in a Cup

The History of Coffee

Coffee has a long and interesting history that connects many cultures around the world. It began as a simple drink made from roasted coffee beans and eventually became part of daily life for millions of people. Over time, coffee shops became places where people gathered to talk, study, work, and share ideas.

Ways to Brew Coffee

There are many different ways to brew coffee, and each method can create a different flavor and experience. Some popular brewing methods include drip coffee, French press, espresso, pour-over, and cold brew. Learning about these methods can help people understand how grind size, water temperature, and brewing time affect the final cup.

Coffee as a Hobby

Coffee brewing and tasting can become a fun and rewarding hobby for anyone who enjoys learning new skills. This website is meant to educate and welcome people who like or love coffee and want to learn more about its history and the many details that make coffee unique. Later in the semester, I may add pages about coffee origins, brewing guides, coffee equipment, drink recipes, tasting notes, and local coffee shop reviews.
