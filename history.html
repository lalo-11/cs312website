Coffee has a rich history that spans centuries and many different cultures. It is believed that coffee was first discovered in Ethiopia before spreading through the Middle East and eventually to Europe and the rest of the world. Today, coffee is one of the most popular beverages and continues to play an important role in social gatherings and daily routines.

Coffee Timeline

  • Coffee is believed to have originated in Ethiopia.
  • Coffee houses became popular gathering places in the Middle East.
  • European coffee culture expanded during the 17th century.
  • Coffee eventually became a global industry enjoyed by millions.

To learn more about the history of coffee, visit the National Coffee Association's History of Coffee page.
